A Tribute to Captain America

Vowing to serve his country any way he could, young Steve Rogers took the super soldier serum to become America's one-man army. Fighting for the red, white and blue for over 60 years, Captain America is the living, breathing symbol of freedom and liberty. A hero for our time, and all time.

A timeline of Captain America moments

  • 1940 Captain America debuts punching Adoph Hitler on the cover of his first comic
  • 1943 Captain America premieres his team of cohorts knowns as the "Howling Commandos"
  • 1945 Captain America disappears while flying near Greenland, and is presumed dead.
  • 2011 Captain America is found frozen but alive in Greenland. He is revived in New York City.
  • 2012 Captain America joins the Avengers Initiative
